You will be given a random number at the start of a game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by amtching your total score to random number.You loose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden till you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change values of each crystals.
